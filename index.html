About Zenora

Zenora produces relaxing music and ambient soundscapes designed for daily wellness routines, including bedtime listening, mindfulness practice, yoga flow, deep work, study sessions, and quiet recovery time.

Zenora publishes original audio-visual content through official brand channels and uses internal publishing tools to prepare metadata, creative assets, and social distribution materials for Zenora-owned content.

What We Create

Sleep Music

Long-form calming music and gentle ambience for nighttime routines, rest, and sleep-focused listening.

Meditation Music

Peaceful soundscapes for breathing, mindfulness, stillness, reflection, and guided personal practice.

Focus Music

Minimal, steady, and low-distraction music for studying, writing, reading, remote work, and creative focus.

Yoga Music

Soft wellness music for stretching, gentle yoga, morning flow, restorative practice, and mindful movement.

Pinterest Publishing Integration

Zenora uses a private internal publishing tool to create, schedule, and publish Pinterest Pins for Zenora-owned wellness music content. The tool is used only by Zenora workflow operators and is not offered as a public app or third-party service.

The integration is intended to:

  • Read Zenora Pinterest boards to select appropriate publishing destinations.
  • Create Pins using Zenora-owned images, titles, descriptions, alt text, and YouTube links.
  • Maintain internal publishing schedules and operational logs for Zenora content.

Zenora does not use this integration to manage ads, collect data from Pinterest users, sell user data, or share user data with third parties.
