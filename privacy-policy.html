Zenora Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Zenora is a wellness music brand that creates calming music content for sleep, meditation, yoga, focus, and relaxation. This Privacy Policy explains how Zenora handles information when using internal publishing tools, including tools used to create, schedule, and publish content to platforms such as Pinterest.

Information We Collect

Zenora does not intentionally collect personal information from Pinterest users through its internal publishing tools.

When Zenora connects to Pinterest, the tool may access account-related information that Pinterest makes available through its API, such as:

  • Pinterest account and profile information needed to authenticate the account
  • Board information needed to select where Pins are published
  • Pin publishing information, including Pin titles, descriptions, images, links, and publishing status
  • Basic reporting or status information related to published Pins, if enabled

Zenora uses this information only to manage and publish Zenora-owned content.

How We Use Information

Zenora uses Pinterest API access only for internal content operations, including:

  • Creating and publishing Pins for Zenora YouTube videos
  • Scheduling Pinterest content
  • Selecting appropriate Pinterest boards
  • Checking whether publishing actions succeeded or failed
  • Maintaining internal publishing logs

Zenora does not sell personal information, rent user data, or use Pinterest API access for advertising profiling.

Content and Links

Zenora Pins may link to Zenora content, including the Zenora website and YouTube channel:

https://www.zenoraambient.com/

https://www.youtube.com/@zenora-e5x

Pinterest users who interact with Pinterest or YouTube are also subject to the privacy policies and terms of those platforms.

Data Sharing

Zenora does not share Pinterest API data with third parties except when necessary to operate official platform integrations or comply with legal obligations.

Data Storage and Security

Zenora stores API credentials and publishing data locally or in secure systems used for internal content management. Access is limited to authorized Zenora workflow operators.

Zenora takes reasonable steps to protect API credentials and publishing records from unauthorized access.

Data Retention

Zenora may keep publishing records, Pin metadata, and scheduling logs for operational and audit purposes. API credentials may be removed or rotated when no longer needed.

User Choices

Pinterest users can manage their privacy settings directly through Pinterest. Users can also disconnect app access from their Pinterest account settings where supported by Pinterest.

Children's Privacy

Zenora's internal publishing tools are not directed to children and are not intended to collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

Zenora may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
